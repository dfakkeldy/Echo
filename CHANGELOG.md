- **Watch timer suspension cap**: When the watch wakes from sleep, `Timer.scheduledTimer` delta is capped at 2.0 seconds ŌĆö beyond that, the watch requests fresh authoritative state from the phone instead of animating through accumulated progress.
- **Watch stale userInfo handling**: After applying received `userInfo` state, the watch immediately requests the phone's current state to converge to the authoritative position.
