Stop choosing between listening and remembering. Echo turns the audiobooks you own into a study system: read along with synced EPUB text, capture the moments that matter, and turn highlights into flashcards you can review on iPhone, Mac, or Apple Watch. No audiobook for that book? Echo narrates text-only EPUB, Markdown, and plain text files on-device, so you can listen and study them too.

Plenty of apps now read an ebook aloud, or follow an audiobook with the text. Echo is the one that turns that into a study system.

Built neurodivergent-first for ADHD, dyslexia, and attention that comes and goes. Echo is a calm, private player for people who learn best by ear.

WHAT MAKES ECHO DIFFERENT
- Read along while you listen: EPUB text follows the audiobook word by word, aligned fully on-device with WhisperKit. No cloud, no account, no tracking.
- No audiobook? Echo narrates a text-only EPUB, Markdown, or plain text file on-device — pick from 28 built-in voices (American and British, female and male), no cloud and no account — then turn that narration into study like any other book. Narration runs on every supported iPhone and Mac (no newest-chip requirement), and a custom pronunciation dictionary teaches it proper nouns and technical terms.
- Full-text search with tap-to-seek: find any sentence in an EPUB and jump straight to that moment in the audio.
- Smart rewind: lose the thread? Echo eases you back a few seconds so an interruption never costs you the chapter.

TURN LISTENING INTO LEARNING
- Highlight a passage and Echo makes a flashcard from it.
- FSRS-4.5 spaced repetition — the modern scheduler Anki now uses — surfaces each card right before you'd forget it.
- Review hands-free on Apple Watch: tap to recall while you walk, commute, or work.
- Import Anki-style flashcard decks from JSON, so your existing study material comes along.

SEE YOUR PROGRESS
- An Insights screen tracks listening time, daily streaks, chapter coverage, and retention, so motivation stays visible.

BUILT FOR FOCUS
- Voice-memo and photo bookmarks pinned to the exact spot.
- Chapter looping to replay a tough passage until it lands.
- Speed control with pitch kept natural, plus a sleep timer for winding down.
- Boost quiet recordings above the system maximum so soft narration stays clear.
- Dyslexia-friendly fonts (Lexend and OpenDyslexic) with full Dynamic Type support.

WORKS WHERE YOU DO
- Import your own DRM-free M4B or MP3 audiobooks, plus EPUB or PDF companion text — EPUB can auto-align, while PDFs support manual page-level alignment for textbooks and scanned references. Your files are read in place, never modified.
- Export the other way too: turn a narrated EPUB, or an audiobook you imported, into a single chaptered .m4b — cover art, metadata, and chapter markers included — playable in any audiobook app.
- Connect to your self-hosted Audiobookshelf server: browse, search, download to your device, and play, with two-way progress sync.
- Import a plain Markdown or .txt file and Echo narrates it as a chaptered audiobook — the same read-along and study tools as an EPUB.
- iPhone, Mac, and Apple Watch, sharing the same library and progress. On Mac, narrate a whole folder of EPUBs overnight, then play or export the results.

OPEN, PRIVATE, NO ADS
Everything runs on your device — alignment and narration included. No account, no tracking, no ads, and your files are never uploaded or modified. Echo is free and open-source under the GPL-3.0 license: import your books, align them, read along, and study. Echo Pro is optional — choose monthly, yearly, or lifetime access for power features like whole-library narration and study export, supporting a solo developer without ever costing you your privacy.
