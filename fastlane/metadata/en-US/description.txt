Stop choosing between listening and remembering. Echo links the audiobook you already own to its EPUB or PDF, syncs every spoken word to the audio on your device, and turns what you hear into what you keep. No audiobook for that book? Echo narrates it on-device in a natural voice — so you can read along and study it either way.

Plenty of apps now read an ebook aloud, or follow an audiobook with the text. Echo is the one that turns that into a study system.

Built neurodivergent-first for ADHD, dyslexia, and attention that comes and goes. Echo is a calm, private player for people who learn best by ear.

WHAT MAKES ECHO DIFFERENT
- Read along while you listen: each word lights up in time with the narration, aligned fully on-device with WhisperKit. No cloud, no account, no tracking.
- No audiobook? Echo narrates a text-only EPUB on-device in a natural voice (no cloud, no account), then reads along and turns it into study like any other book.
- Full-text search with tap-to-seek: find any sentence and jump straight to that moment in the audio.
- Smart rewind: lose the thread? Echo eases you back a few seconds so an interruption never costs you the chapter.

TURN LISTENING INTO LEARNING
- Highlight a passage and Echo makes a flashcard from it.
- SM-2 spaced repetition schedules each card so the right one resurfaces at the right time.
- Review hands-free on Apple Watch: tap to recall while you walk, commute, or work.
- Import Anki-style flashcard decks from JSON, so your existing study material comes along.

SEE YOUR PROGRESS
- An Insights screen tracks listening time, daily streaks, chapter coverage, and retention, so motivation stays visible.

BUILT FOR FOCUS
- Voice-memo and photo bookmarks pinned to the exact spot.
- Chapter looping to replay a tough passage until it lands.
- Speed control with pitch kept natural, plus a sleep timer for winding down.
- Dyslexia-friendly fonts (Lexend and OpenDyslexic) with full Dynamic Type support.

WORKS WHERE YOU DO
- Bring your own M4B audiobooks and EPUB or PDF companion text.
- iPhone, Mac, and Apple Watch, sharing the same library and progress.

OPEN, PRIVATE, NO ADS
Everything runs on your device — alignment and narration included. No account, no tracking, no ads, and your files are never uploaded or modified. Echo is free and open-source under the GPL-3.0 license: import your books, align them, read along, and study. Echo Pro is an optional upgrade — a subscription or a one-time unlock — that adds power features like whole-library narration and study export, supporting a solo developer without ever costing you your privacy.
